F.A.Q

  • Is there a YouTube channel?

    Link youtube.com/@koopamaster

  • Do you work with Nintendo?

    Nope. This game is a modification (a mod) of Mario Kart DS, that brings Mario Kart Wii to the DS.
    No support of any kind from Nintendo.

  • Do you plan to port all assets?

    Not all,the DS and MKDS itself have limitations.
    At least, all the 32 tracks and battle tracks + some of the karts and some chracters swaps will be done (+ Audio and UI of course!)

  • Is this mod gonna be finished?

    We're not sure at 100%, but there's and there will still be progress.

  • Release date?

    Not fixed now. Of course a new demo date will be fixed soon, we guess.

  • Do you have a Discord?

    Of course, you'll be able to get more leaks there: Discord server

  • Will you make Knuckles?

    No. It's Mario Kart WII on DS.
    People really asked for that...

Mixing them together, by "remaking" almost all the assets on the small handheld game console is our goal, but not a simple job.
For now, a very old demo and a "fresher" micro-demo are avalaible to download.
Please make sure to follow all instructions.
